University Student Support Chatbot - QA Test Cases
==================================================

Test 1: Admissions requirements
Input: What are the admission requirements?
Expected: Mentions application, transcripts, and GPA requirements.

Test 2: Application process
Input: How do I apply for admission?
Expected: Mentions online application portal and required documents.

Test 3: Financial aid
Input: How do I apply for financial aid?
Expected: Mentions FAFSA and financial aid package.

Test 4: Scholarships
Input: When is the scholarship deadline?
Expected: Mentions March 15 or directs to financial aid office.

Test 5: Class registration
Input: How do I register for classes?
Expected: Mentions student portal and registration window.

Test 6: Student services
Input: What support services are available?
Expected: Mentions advising, tutoring, career services, or counseling.

Test 7: Out-of-scope query
Input: What is the weather today?
Expected: Fallback message indicating no confident FAQ match.

Test 8: Rephrased question
Input: Can I get help choosing my courses?
Expected: Mentions academic advising or student support services.

Test 9: International students
Input: What do international students need for admission?
Expected: Mentions English proficiency and visa-related requirements.

Test 10: Source citation
Input: What scholarships are available?
Expected: Answer includes a Sources section with FAQ references.
